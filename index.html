Welcome to PartyBus VA

Welcome to the PartyBus Virtual Airline documentation. This site contains all the information you need to get started and operate as a pilot with our virtual airline.

Last Updated: March 15, 2008

Getting Started
Requirements

To join PartyBus VA, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Flight simulator software (FS2004, FSX, or newer)
  • Internet connection
  • Basic understanding of aviation procedures
  • Membership application submitted
Joining Process

To join our virtual airline, follow these steps:

  1. Download the membership application form
  2. Fill out all required fields
  3. Email the completed form to applications@partybusva.com
  4. Wait for approval (usually 2-3 business days)
  5. Receive your pilot ID and login credentials
  6. Complete the orientation flight
Note: Applications are reviewed manually by our staff. Please allow adequate time for processing.
Rules and Regulations
General Rules

All pilots must adhere to the following rules:

# Rule
1 All flights must be logged using the ACARS system
2 Minimum of 3 flights per month required to maintain active status
3 Follow all ATC instructions when online
4 Use proper radio communications
5 No cheating or unrealistic flight parameters
6 Respect other pilots and staff members
7 Report technical issues to the IT department
Important: Violations of these rules may result in suspension or termination of membership.

Operations
Flight Scheduling

Pilots can schedule flights through the online system. Available routes are listed in the flight database.

ACARS System

All flights must be logged using the ACARS system. The system tracks:

  • Flight duration
  • Distance flown
  • Fuel consumption
  • Landing rate
  • Flight path
ACARS Connection Settings:
Server: acars.partybusva.com
Port: 6809
Protocol: FSPassengers
Update Interval: 30 seconds
Flight Hours

Flight hours are automatically calculated and logged. Pilots can view their flight hours in their pilot profile.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or contact technical support.

Q: What happens if I don't meet the minimum flight requirement?

A: Pilots who do not meet the minimum flight requirement may be placed on inactive status. Contact the operations manager to discuss your situation.

Q: Can I fly routes not in the database?

A: Custom routes may be approved on a case-by-case basis. Submit a request through the forum or contact operations.

Q: How do I report a bug in the ACARS system?

A: Send detailed information about the bug to technical support, including screenshots if possible.

Q: When are new routes added?

A: New routes are typically added monthly. Check the forum announcements for updates.

Q: Can I join multiple virtual airlines?

A: Yes, but please ensure you can meet the requirements for all airlines you join.
