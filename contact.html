Contact Information

This page provides contact information for various departments within PartyBus Virtual Airline. Use the appropriate contact method for your inquiry.

Department Contacts
Department Email Address Purpose
General Inquiries info@partybusva.com General questions about PartyBus VA
Technical Support support@partybusva.com ACARS issues, technical problems, system errors
Operations ops@partybusva.com Flight operations, route questions, scheduling
Fleet Management fleet@partybusva.com Aircraft configurations, paint kit questions
Training training@partybusva.com Training programs, orientation flight questions
Administration admin@partybusva.com Administrative matters, account issues
Response Times

PartyBus VA staff members respond to inquiries according to the following schedule:

  • Technical Support: Within 24 hours (urgent issues prioritized)
  • Operations: Within 48 hours
  • General Inquiries: Within 48-72 hours
  • Fleet Management: Within 72 hours
  • Training: Within 48 hours
For Urgent Matters: If you have an urgent technical issue preventing flight operations, include "URGENT" in your subject line and contact technical support immediately.
Email Guidelines

When contacting PartyBus VA staff, please follow these guidelines:

  • Use a descriptive subject line indicating the nature of your inquiry
  • Include your pilot ID if applicable
  • Provide detailed information about your issue or question
  • For technical issues, include error messages and screenshots if possible
  • Be patient - staff members are volunteers and respond as quickly as possible
Other Communication Channels

In addition to email, PartyBus VA maintains other communication channels:

  • Forum: Visit the PartyBus VA Forum for community discussions and support
  • Documentation: Many questions can be answered by consulting the documentation on this site
  • FAQ: Check the Frequently Asked Questions page for common questions
Office Hours

PartyBus VA staff are generally available during the following hours (Eastern Time):

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM EST
  • Sunday: Closed

Note: These are general availability hours. Responses may still be sent outside these hours, but cannot be guaranteed.

Tip: Before contacting staff, please review the relevant documentation sections. Many questions can be answered quickly by consulting the Operations Manual, Pilot Handbook, or FAQ section.
