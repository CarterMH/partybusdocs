Code of Conduct

Document Number: COC-PBVA-001
Effective Date: March 1, 2008
Applicable to: All PartyBus Virtual Airline pilots, staff, and members

The PartyBus Virtual Airline Code of Conduct establishes the standards of behavior expected from all members of our organization. This code applies to all interactions, communications, and activities related to PartyBus VA operations.

Mandatory Compliance: All members must comply with this Code of Conduct. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including suspension or termination of membership.
1. Professionalism

1.1 General Conduct

All members shall:

  • Conduct themselves in a professional and respectful manner at all times
  • Maintain appropriate behavior in all communications and interactions
  • Treat all members, staff, and external contacts with respect and courtesy
  • Represent PartyBus VA in a positive and professional manner
  • Refrain from behavior that could reflect negatively on the organization

1.2 Communication Standards

All communications must:

  • Be professional and respectful in tone and content
  • Avoid offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language
  • Use proper aviation terminology and phraseology
  • Refrain from personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
  • Maintain confidentiality when appropriate

1.3 Appearance and Presentation

  • Use appropriate call signs and identifiers
  • Maintain proper aircraft liveries and markings
  • Follow standardized procedures and checklists
  • Present flight data and reports in a professional manner
2. Integrity and Honesty

2.1 Truthfulness

Members must:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information in all reports and communications
  • Not falsify flight data, logs, or records
  • Not misrepresent qualifications, experience, or certifications
  • Report errors, mistakes, and discrepancies honestly

2.2 Flight Data Integrity

  • Submit only accurate flight data through ACARS
  • Not manipulate or falsify flight parameters
  • Report technical issues that may affect data accuracy
  • Maintain integrity of flight logs and records

2.3 Account Security

  • Maintain security of pilot ID and account credentials
  • Not share accounts or pilot IDs with others
  • Report any unauthorized access immediately
  • Use accounts only for authorized purposes
3. Respect for Others

3.1 Equal Treatment

All members shall:

  • Treat all individuals equally regardless of background, experience level, or other characteristics
  • Not engage in discrimination, harassment, or bias of any kind
  • Respect differing opinions and perspectives
  • Provide assistance and support to fellow members when appropriate

3.2 Harassment Prohibition

The following behaviors are prohibited:

  • Verbal harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Inappropriate comments or conduct
  • Unwanted attention or contact
  • Bullying or persistent negative behavior
  • Any conduct that creates a hostile environment

3.3 Conflict Resolution

  • Address disagreements in a respectful and constructive manner
  • Seek resolution through appropriate channels when conflicts arise
  • Refrain from public disputes or arguments
  • Involve staff members when necessary to resolve issues
4. Safety and Operations

4.1 Flight Safety

Members must:

  • Prioritize safety in all flight operations
  • Follow all procedures, checklists, and SOPs
  • Not engage in unsafe or reckless flight operations
  • Report safety concerns or hazards immediately
  • Maintain proper flight parameters and procedures

4.2 Operational Standards

  • Adhere to all operational procedures and requirements
  • Complete flights according to approved procedures
  • Maintain realistic flight profiles and parameters
  • Follow ATC instructions when flying online
  • Comply with all rules and regulations

4.3 Equipment and Systems

  • Use approved aircraft configurations and systems
  • Ensure systems are properly configured before flight
  • Report technical issues or malfunctions
  • Not use unauthorized modifications or software
5. Responsibility and Accountability

5.1 Personal Responsibility

Members are responsible for:

  • Their own actions and decisions
  • Compliance with all rules and procedures
  • Maintaining required activity levels
  • Accurate reporting of flight data
  • Professional conduct in all interactions

5.2 Reporting Obligations

  • Report rule violations or misconduct
  • Report safety concerns or hazards
  • Report technical issues or problems
  • Self-report errors or mistakes when appropriate

5.3 Accountability for Actions

  • Accept responsibility for violations or errors
  • Participate in corrective action when required
  • Comply with disciplinary decisions
  • Learn from mistakes and improve performance
6. Privacy and Confidentiality

6.1 Information Privacy

Members must:

  • Respect privacy of other members
  • Not disclose confidential information without authorization
  • Use information only for authorized purposes
  • Protect personal information of others

6.2 Confidential Communications

  • Respect confidentiality of internal communications
  • Not share private discussions without permission
  • Handle sensitive information appropriately
7. Technology and Systems Use

7.1 Authorized Use

  • Use PartyBus VA systems only for authorized purposes
  • Not attempt to circumvent security measures
  • Not use systems to harm or interfere with operations
  • Report security vulnerabilities or concerns

7.2 Software and Modifications

  • Use only approved aircraft configurations
  • Not use unauthorized modifications or hacks
  • Not manipulate systems to gain unfair advantages
  • Comply with software licensing requirements
8. Activity and Participation

8.1 Activity Requirements

  • Maintain minimum activity requirements (3 flights per month)
  • Complete flights according to procedures
  • Log all flights accurately through ACARS
  • Respond to communications in a timely manner

8.2 Participation

  • Participate constructively in community activities
  • Contribute positively to discussions and forums
  • Support training and development activities
  • Assist new members when possible
9. Compliance and Enforcement

9.1 Compliance

All members must:

  • Read and understand this Code of Conduct
  • Comply with all provisions of this code
  • Accept that violations may result in disciplinary action
  • Cooperate with investigations of alleged violations

9.2 Reporting Violations

Violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to:

  • Operations Manager (ops@partybus.org) for operational violations
  • Administration (admin@partybus.org) for conduct violations
  • Through appropriate reporting channels as established

9.3 Disciplinary Action

Violations may result in:

  • Verbal warning
  • Written warning
  • Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
  • Permanent suspension
  • Termination of membership

9.4 Appeals Process

Members have the right to:

  • Appeal disciplinary decisions
  • Present their case to appropriate authorities
  • Request review of disciplinary actions
10. Amendments and Updates

This Code of Conduct may be updated periodically. All members will be notified of changes. Continued membership constitutes acceptance of the current Code of Conduct.

Acknowledgment: By remaining a member of PartyBus Virtual Airline, all pilots acknowledge they have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Code of Conduct.
