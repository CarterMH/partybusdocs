Fleet Information

PartyBus Virtual Airline operates an exclusive fleet of Airbus A320 family aircraft in the CEO (Current Engine Option) variant. All aircraft feature the PartyBus VA custom livery and are configured for optimal performance across our route network.

Fleet Overview

Our fleet consists of three aircraft variants, all from the Airbus A320 family CEO series:

Aircraft Type ICAO Code Status
Airbus A319 Narrow-body Airliner (CEO) A319 Active
Airbus A320 Narrow-body Airliner (CEO) A320 Active
Airbus A321 Narrow-body Airliner (CEO) A321 Active
Airbus A319 Specifications

The Airbus A319 is the smallest variant in our fleet, optimized for short to medium-haul routes.

General Characteristics:

  • Length: 33.84 meters (111.0 feet)
  • Wingspan: 34.10 meters (111.9 feet)
  • Height: 11.76 meters (38.6 feet)
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 75,500 kg (166,450 lbs)
  • Maximum Landing Weight: 66,000 kg (145,500 lbs)

Performance Specifications:

  • Range: 6,850 km (3,700 nm) with typical load
  • Maximum Cruise Speed: Mach 0.82 (470 knots / 871 km/h)
  • Typical Cruise Speed: Mach 0.78 (448 knots / 830 km/h)
  • Service Ceiling: 39,000 feet (FL390)
  • Takeoff Distance: 1,950 meters (6,400 feet) at MTOW
  • Landing Distance: 1,530 meters (5,020 feet) at MLW

Capacity:

  • Typical Seating: 124-156 passengers (two-class configuration)
  • Maximum Seating: 156 passengers (single-class, high-density)
  • Cargo Capacity: 27.6 cubic meters (975 cubic feet)

Engines:

  • Engine Options: CFM56-5B or IAE V2500
  • Thrust Range: 22,000 - 27,000 lbf per engine

Recommended Routes:

  • Short-haul routes: 200-800 nm
  • Medium-haul routes: 800-1,200 nm
  • Ideal for regional and domestic operations

For aircraft configuration files, see Aircraft Configurations.

Airbus A320 Specifications

The Airbus A320 is the standard variant in our fleet, serving as the backbone of our operations.

General Characteristics:

  • Length: 37.57 meters (123.3 feet)
  • Wingspan: 34.10 meters (111.9 feet)
  • Height: 11.76 meters (38.6 feet)
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 78,000 kg (171,960 lbs)
  • Maximum Landing Weight: 66,000 kg (145,500 lbs)

Performance Specifications:

  • Range: 6,100 km (3,300 nm) with typical load
  • Maximum Cruise Speed: Mach 0.82 (470 knots / 871 km/h)
  • Typical Cruise Speed: Mach 0.78 (448 knots / 830 km/h)
  • Service Ceiling: 39,000 feet (FL390)
  • Takeoff Distance: 2,090 meters (6,860 feet) at MTOW
  • Landing Distance: 1,530 meters (5,020 feet) at MLW

Capacity:

  • Typical Seating: 150-180 passengers (two-class configuration)
  • Maximum Seating: 180 passengers (single-class, high-density)
  • Cargo Capacity: 37.4 cubic meters (1,320 cubic feet)

Engines:

  • Engine Options: CFM56-5B or IAE V2500
  • Thrust Range: 22,000 - 27,000 lbf per engine

Recommended Routes:

  • Medium-haul routes: 400-1,500 nm
  • Short to medium-haul operations
  • Most versatile aircraft in the fleet
Airbus A321 Specifications

The Airbus A321 is the largest variant in our fleet, optimized for medium to long-haul routes with higher capacity.

General Characteristics:

  • Length: 44.51 meters (146.0 feet)
  • Wingspan: 34.10 meters (111.9 feet)
  • Height: 11.76 meters (38.6 feet)
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 93,500 kg (206,130 lbs)
  • Maximum Landing Weight: 77,800 kg (171,520 lbs)

Performance Specifications:

  • Range: 5,950 km (3,200 nm) with typical load
  • Maximum Cruise Speed: Mach 0.82 (470 knots / 871 km/h)
  • Typical Cruise Speed: Mach 0.78 (448 knots / 830 km/h)
  • Service Ceiling: 39,000 feet (FL390)
  • Takeoff Distance: 2,180 meters (7,150 feet) at MTOW
  • Landing Distance: 1,830 meters (6,000 feet) at MLW

Capacity:

  • Typical Seating: 185-220 passengers (two-class configuration)
  • Maximum Seating: 220 passengers (single-class, high-density)
  • Cargo Capacity: 51.8 cubic meters (1,830 cubic feet)

Engines:

  • Engine Options: CFM56-5B or IAE V2530
  • Thrust Range: 31,000 - 33,000 lbf per engine

Recommended Routes:

  • Medium to long-haul routes: 600-2,500 nm
  • High-capacity routes requiring more seats
  • Longer stage lengths
