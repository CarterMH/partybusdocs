Orientation Flight Procedures

All new pilots must complete an orientation flight before flying regular routes.

Required Orientation Route

Route: KSEA - KPDX (Seattle to Portland)
Aircraft: Airbus A319, A320, or A321 (CEO)
Distance: 129 nm

Flight Checklist
  1. Review the Operations Manual
  2. Set up your ACARS system (see ACARS Guide)
  3. Load the aircraft configuration
  4. Complete pre-flight checks
  5. File a flight plan
  6. Execute the flight
  7. Submit flight report via ACARS
Important: The orientation flight must be completed within 7 days of account activation.
After Completion

Once you complete the orientation flight, your account will be fully activated and you can begin flying regular routes.
