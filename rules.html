Rules and Regulations
Important: All pilots must read and agree to these rules. Violations may result in suspension or termination of membership.
General Rules

All pilots must adhere to the following rules:

# Rule
1 All flights must be logged using the ACARS system
2 Minimum of 3 flights per month required to maintain active status
3 Follow all ATC instructions when online
4 Use proper radio communications
5 No cheating or unrealistic flight parameters
6 Respect other pilots and staff members
7 Report technical issues to the IT department
8 Do not share your pilot ID or login credentials
9 Complete flights must follow the planned route
10 Maintain realistic flight times and speeds
Flight Conduct Rules
  • Pilots must maintain realistic flight profiles
  • No excessive speeds or unrealistic maneuvers
  • Proper takeoff and landing procedures must be followed
  • ATC instructions must be followed when online
  • Radio communications must be professional
Activity Requirements

To maintain active status, pilots must:

  • Complete at least 3 flights per month
  • Log flights using the ACARS system
  • Respond to communications from staff within 7 days
Note: Pilots who do not meet activity requirements will be placed on inactive status. Contact the operations manager to discuss exceptions.
Disciplinary Actions

Violations of these rules may result in:

  1. Verbal warning
  2. Written warning
  3. Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
  4. Permanent suspension
Appeals Process

If you feel a disciplinary action was unjustified, you may appeal by contacting the operations manager at ops@partybusva.com.
