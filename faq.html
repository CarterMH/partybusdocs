Frequently Asked Questions
General Questions

Q: How do I reset my password? [Show/Hide]

A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or contact technical support at support@partybusva.com.

Q: What happens if I don't meet the minimum flight requirement? [Show/Hide]

A: Pilots who do not meet the minimum flight requirement (3 flights per month) may be placed on inactive status. Contact the operations manager to discuss your situation and request an exception.

Q: Can I fly routes not in the database? [Show/Hide]

A: Custom routes may be approved on a case-by-case basis. Submit a request through the forum or contact operations at ops@partybusva.com.

Q: How do I report a bug in the ACARS system? [Show/Hide]

A: Send detailed information about the bug to technical support at support@partybusva.com, including screenshots if possible. For ACARS-specific issues, see the ACARS System Guide.

Q: When are new routes added? [Show/Hide]

A: New routes are typically added monthly. Check the forum announcements for updates. You can also download the latest Route Database.

Q: Can I join multiple virtual airlines? [Show/Hide]

A: Yes, but please ensure you can meet the requirements for all airlines you join. You must use separate pilot IDs for each airline.

Technical Questions

Q: Which flight simulators are supported? [Show/Hide]

A: We support Microsoft Flight Simulator 2004, Flight Simulator X, and X-Plane. ACARS compatibility varies by simulator version.

Q: How do I install aircraft liveries? [Show/Hide]

A: Download the Paint Kit and follow the installation instructions included. For detailed instructions, contact fleet management.
